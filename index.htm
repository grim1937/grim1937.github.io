8-Bit Wonder World

Hi! We're your friendly neighborhood 8-Bit Wonderland game store!
We buy and sell retro, import and modern video games, systems and accessories.
We also carry gaming related toys, figures, clothing, as well as a vast selection of comics.

Founded in 1964, 8-Bit Wonderland began as a small toy shop in New York, New York by Jim and Katy Harris.
Through the years, they developed a vast market along the east coast that still is still growing today.
With the love of video games and strong business drive, 8-Bit hopes to continue to bring joy to all gamers for years to come.
